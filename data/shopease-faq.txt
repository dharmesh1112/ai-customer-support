## Returns & Exchanges

### How do I return a product?
To initiate a return on ShopEase:
1. Go to My Orders → Select the order
2. Click "Return Item" next to the product
3. Choose your reason for return (defective, wrong item, didn't like it, etc.)
4. Select pickup or drop-off:
   - **Pickup**: Our courier will collect from your address within 2-3 business days
   - **Drop-off**: Take it to any ShopEase partner store (find locations in the app)
5. Pack the item in its original packaging with all tags attached
6. You'll receive a confirmation email with your return tracking ID

Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery for most products. Electronics have a 10-day window.

### What items cannot be returned?
The following items are NOT eligible for return:
- Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
- Customized/personalized products
- Perfumes and beauty products (if opened/used)
- Digital downloads, gift cards, and subscriptions
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, fresh items)
- Items marked "Non-Returnable" on the product page

### How do exchanges work?
Exchange is available for clothing and footwear only:
1. Go to My Orders → Click "Exchange" on the item
2. Select the new size/color you want
3. Schedule a pickup for the original item
4. The replacement ships once we receive and verify your original item (typically 3-5 business days after pickup)

Note: Exchange is only for size/color changes, NOT for a different product. Price differences (if any) will be charged or refunded to your original payment method.

### What condition must returned items be in?
To qualify for a return or exchange:
- Item must be unused, unwashed, and undamaged
- All original tags, labels, and packaging must be intact
- Accessories, freebies, and combo items must be returned together
- Electronics must include all original cables, chargers, and manuals
- Furniture must be unassembled (if delivered in parts)

If the item fails our quality check, it will be shipped back to you at no cost and your refund will not be processed.

## Refunds

### How long does a refund take?
Refund timelines after we receive and verify your returned item:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---------------|----------------|
| UPI / Wallet | 24-48 hours |
| Credit Card | 5-7 business days |
| Debit Card | 5-10 business days |
| Net Banking | 7-10 business days |
| Cash on Delivery | 3-5 business days (credited to ShopEase Wallet) |

You'll receive an email and SMS notification once your refund is initiated.

### Where will my refund go?
- **Prepaid orders (UPI, card, net banking)**: Refund goes back to the original payment method used during purchase.
- **Cash on Delivery orders**: Refund is credited to your ShopEase Wallet. You can use it for future purchases or request a bank transfer (takes 3-5 additional days).
- **Partial COD + Wallet orders**: The wallet portion refunds to wallet instantly, the COD portion goes to ShopEase Wallet.

### Can I get a refund to a different account?
No — for security purposes, refunds are always processed to the original payment source. If your card has expired or account is closed, contact your bank — they'll route it to your updated account automatically.

### What if my refund is delayed?
If your refund hasn't arrived within the expected timeline:
1. Check your email for a "Refund Initiated" confirmation
2. If confirmed, wait 2-3 extra business days (bank processing)
3. If still missing, go to Help → Refund Status → Enter Order ID
4. Contact our support team via chat with your order number and refund reference ID

Common reasons for delays: bank holidays, incorrect bank details on file, pending verification from payment gateway.

## Orders & Tracking

### How do I track my order?
You can track your order in multiple ways:
- **App/Website**: My Orders → Click on the order → "Track Shipment"
- **SMS/Email**: You receive tracking updates at every stage (ordered, shipped, out for delivery, delivered)
- **WhatsApp**: Send your order number to +91-9876543210 for instant status
- **Delivery partner app**: Once shipped, you'll get a link to the courier's tracking page (Delhivery, BlueDart, Ecom Express, etc.)

Tracking updates are refreshed every 2-4 hours.

### Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
- **Before shipment**: Yes! Go to My Orders → Order Details → "Edit Address" (available until the order is packed)
- **After shipment**: Not possible. You'll need to refuse delivery and place a new order, or accept it and initiate a return.

### Can I cancel an order?
- **Before shipment**: Yes, full cancellation with instant refund. Go to My Orders → "Cancel Order"
- **After shipment**: You cannot cancel, but you can refuse delivery when it arrives (refund processed within 7-10 days)
- **Partially shipped**: Individual items can be cancelled if not yet shipped

Cancellation charges:
- No charges for cancellation before shipment
- ₹50 convenience fee for COD orders cancelled after shipment
- Made-to-order/customized items cannot be cancelled once production starts

### What if I receive a damaged or wrong item?
If your item arrived damaged, defective, or is the wrong product:
1. Do NOT use or alter the item
2. Take photos/video of the damage (including packaging)
3. Go to My Orders → Report Issue → Select "Damaged" or "Wrong Item"
4. Upload your photos as evidence
5. We'll arrange a pickup within 24 hours (no need to return the item for damages under ₹500 — we'll just refund)
6. Replacement ships within 2-3 business days, or choose full refund

Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery for fastest resolution.

### What is the estimated delivery time?
Delivery timelines depend on your location and the seller:

| Service | Timeline | Cost |
|---------|----------|------|
| Standard Delivery | 4-7 business days | Free (orders above ₹499) |
| Express Delivery | 1-2 business days | ₹99 (or free for ShopEase Plus members) |
| Same-Day Delivery | By 9 PM today (order before 12 PM) | ₹149 (select cities only: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad) |
| Scheduled Delivery | Choose your date (up to 7 days ahead) | Free |

Note: Delivery times may be affected during sales events, festivals, and extreme weather conditions. Remote pin codes may take 2-3 additional days.

## Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
ShopEase accepts:
- **UPI**: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
- **Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, Rupay, Amex (credit & debit)
- **Net Banking**: All major Indian banks
- **Wallets**: ShopEase Wallet, Paytm Wallet, Amazon Pay
- **EMI**: No-cost EMI on select products (3/6/9/12 months)
- **Cash on Delivery**: Available for orders under ₹25,000
- **Buy Now Pay Later**: LazyPay, Simpl, ZestMoney (for eligible users)

### Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
COD is available for most products under ₹25,000. However, COD is NOT available for:
- Orders above ₹25,000
- Digital products (gift cards, subscriptions)
- Certain high-value electronics
- Addresses with previous failed COD deliveries
- First-time buyers with unverified phone numbers (for security)

COD handling fee: ₹19 per order (waived for ShopEase Plus members).

### What is ShopEase Wallet?
ShopEase Wallet is our in-app digital wallet:
- Add money via UPI, card, or net banking
- Get instant refunds credited here (no waiting for bank processing)
- Earn 2% cashback on wallet top-ups during promotions
- Use alongside other payment methods (split payment)
- Maximum wallet balance: ₹50,000
- No expiry on wallet balance

### My payment failed but money was deducted — what do I do?
Don't worry — this is usually a temporary hold by your bank:
1. Wait 30 minutes and check your bank statement again (pending charges often auto-reverse)
2. If the amount is still debited after 24 hours, check your email for an order confirmation:
   - **Order confirmed**: Your payment went through successfully!
   - **No order confirmation**: The amount will auto-refund within 5-7 business days
3. If no auto-refund after 7 days, contact support with your transaction reference number and bank statement screenshot

### How does No-Cost EMI work?
On select products marked "No Cost EMI":
- The interest is borne by the seller/ShopEase (you pay exactly the product price, split into installments)
- Available on credit cards from HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak
- Tenure options: 3, 6, 9, or 12 months
- Minimum cart value: ₹3,000
- Processing fee (₹199-₹499) may apply on some banks — shown at checkout before payment

## Account & Membership

### How do I create an account?
Sign up on ShopEase using:
- Mobile number + OTP (quickest method)
- Email address + password
- Google or Facebook sign-in

After signing up, verify your email to unlock all features (wishlist sync, order emails, exclusive offers).

### What is ShopEase Plus?
ShopEase Plus is our premium membership program (₹999/year or ₹99/month):
- **Free Express Delivery** on ALL orders (no minimum)
- **Early access** to sales (24 hours before everyone else)
- **Extra 5% off** on select brands
- **Free same-day delivery** in 4 metro cities
- **Priority customer support** (dedicated queue, <2 min wait time)
- **No COD charges**
- **Exclusive member-only deals** every week

Cancel anytime — pro-rated refund for unused months.

### I forgot my password — how do I reset it?
1. Click "Login" → "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your registered mobile number or email
3. You'll receive an OTP (valid for 5 minutes)
4. Enter the OTP and set a new password
5. Password requirements: minimum 8 characters, at least one number and one special character

If you don't receive the OTP, try switching between SMS and email delivery. Still stuck? Contact support with your registered email for manual verification.

### How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your ShopEase account:
1. Go to Settings → Account → "Delete My Account"
2. Enter your password to confirm
3. You'll have a 30-day cooling-off period (can reactivate by logging in)
4. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted

Before deleting: download your order history, redeem any wallet balance, and cancel active ShopEase Plus membership.

## Shipping & Delivery

### Do you deliver across India?
Yes! ShopEase delivers to 19,000+ pin codes across India, including:
- All state capitals and tier-1 cities (1-3 day delivery)
- Tier-2 and tier-3 cities (3-5 day delivery)
- Rural areas and remote pin codes (5-10 day delivery)
- Northeast states and island territories (7-12 day delivery)

Check delivery availability by entering your pin code on any product page.

### What happens if I'm not home during delivery?
If you're not available during delivery:
1. The delivery partner will call you (the number on your order)
2. If unreachable, they'll attempt delivery again the next day
3. After 3 failed attempts, the order is returned to the warehouse
4. You'll be notified via SMS/email before each attempt
5. For express/same-day orders: you can reschedule via the tracking link

Tip: Add delivery instructions in your address ("Leave with security guard", "Call before arriving", etc.)

### Is delivery free?
- **Standard Delivery**: Free on orders above ₹499
- Orders below ₹499: ₹40 delivery fee
- **ShopEase Plus members**: Always free (no minimum)
- **Seller-specific shipping**: Some sellers charge their own shipping — shown on product page

### How is my package packaged?
We take care of your items:
- **Fragile items**: Bubble wrap + sturdy corrugated box + "FRAGILE" label
- **Clothing**: Polybag with air cushion (water-resistant)
- **Electronics**: Original brand packaging + outer box + foam padding
- **Large items (furniture, appliances)**: White-glove delivery with unpacking service included

## Offers & Coupons

### How do I apply a coupon?
1. Add items to your cart
2. Go to checkout → Look for "Apply Coupon" field
3. Enter your coupon code (case-sensitive) OR browse available coupons by clicking "View All Coupons"
4. Click "Apply" — the discount reflects in your order total

Only one coupon can be applied per order. Bank offers (10% off on HDFC, etc.) stack with coupons.

### Why is my coupon not working?
Common reasons:
- Coupon has expired (check validity date)
- Minimum purchase amount not met
- Coupon is for specific categories only (e.g., "FASHION20" only works on fashion items)
- Product is already on sale (coupons may not stack with sale prices)
- Coupon is for first-time users only and you've ordered before
- Maximum discount cap reached (e.g., "50% off up to ₹200")

### What is ShopEase SuperCoins?
SuperCoins is our rewards program:
- Earn 1 SuperCoin per ₹100 spent
- ShopEase Plus members earn 2x SuperCoins
- Redeem: 1 SuperCoin = ₹1 discount
- Use SuperCoins for exclusive deals in "Rewards Store"
- SuperCoins expire 12 months after earning (on a rolling basis)
- Check balance: My Account → SuperCoins
